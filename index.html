Baofeng Chang / 常宝峰

I'm a Ph.D. student in ZJUTVis Lab, College of Computer Science and Technology, Zhejiang University of Technology, Hangzhou, China, where I'm working with Prof. Guodao Sun and Prof. Ronghua Liang.

My research interest mainly lies on Dynamic Graph Visualization, Temporal Sequence Visualization, and Traffic Visualization. My research scenario involves sharing bike, music, sports, etc.

I received bachelor's degree in Automation from College of Information Engineering, Zhejiang University of Technology in 2017. Now, I'm doing with doctoral degree in Computer Science and Technology at College of Computer Science and Technology, Zhejiang University of Technology. I'm looking forward collaboration and interchange in visual analysis.

Ongoing Research

  • Dynamic graph visualization--Hierarchical snapshot extraction for time-intensive dynamic graph data.
  • Zone division in city--visual analysis method of constrained zone division method.
  • How to persuade others--Visual analysis of expression, emotion, and other human factors.
  • Video visual analysis--Visual analysis of video towards human behavior and event.
